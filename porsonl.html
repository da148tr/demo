About Me

Hello! I'm DANIEL TSIGU, a dedicated professional with a strong background in web development and digital design. Based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, I specialize in creating responsive, user-friendly, and engaging web experiences.

My journey began 5 years ago, driven by a passion for technology and a desire to build innovative solutions. I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various modern frameworks. I also have a keen eye for UI/UX principles, ensuring that every project I undertake is not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing and intuitive.
